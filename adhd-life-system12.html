🔁 The "Never Miss Twice" Rule

The goal of this system isn't a perfect streak. It's to make starting again as easy as possible.

When you miss a day, don't try to catch up. Don't guilt yourself. Just open this and begin the next block that makes sense for the time you have right now.

You are not starting over. You are continuing.

🧠 ADHD Survival Toolkit
Quick wins for dysregulated moments
  • 🌀 Body doubling: Put on a "study with me" or "work with me" YouTube video — your brain thinks you're not alone.
  • ⏱️ The 2-minute rule: If it takes under 2 minutes, do it immediately. This kills 80% of pile-up.
  • 📍 Body first: Overwhelmed? Move your body before you try to think. Even 1 minute.
  • 🎵 Music as a cue: One playlist = focus mode. Same playlist every time trains your brain.
  • 📋 Visible task board: If you can't see it, it doesn't exist. Keep a sticky note with today's 3 must-dos physically visible.

🧠 ADHD & Weight Loss: The Real Talk
🍽️ Skipping meals = binge trigger. ADHD brains get hyperfocused and forget to eat, then hit starving and eat everything. Set a phone alarm for meals.
🎯 Protein at every meal. Protein keeps you full longer and stabilizes dopamine. Eggs, Greek yogurt, chicken, cheese — make it easy to grab.
🧃 Liquid calories sneak up on you. Juice, fancy coffees, sodas — ADHD impulsivity + liquid calories is a hidden pattern. Swap one per day for water.
🚶 "Exercise" is too big a word. Start with 10-minute walks. Movement after meals specifically helps blood sugar AND dopamine. You don't need a gym.
😴 Sleep deprivation tanks your progress. ADHD + poor sleep raises hunger hormones dramatically. Getting kids to bed on time protects YOUR weight loss too.
🍫 Impulsive eating is ADHD, not weakness. Keep trigger foods out of the house. If it's not there, your impulsive brain can't eat it. Stock what you want to eat instead.
📸 Take progress photos, not just weights. ADHD brains need visual, immediate feedback. Photos show changes the scale misses — especially with building muscle.

💡 ADHD Movement Truths
👟 Shoes on = workout happening. The hardest part is putting your shoes on. Do that first. Everything else follows.
⏱️ 10 minutes still counts. ADHD brains need to lower the bar, not raise it. 10 min of movement beats 0 min of "I'll do it later."

🔄 Same time, same place. ADHD thrives on external structure. Pick one spot in your house and always work out there.
💜 Movement is ADHD medication. Literally. Exercise increases dopamine and norepinephrine — the same chemicals your meds target. It's not optional, it's medicine.

💡 1200 Cal ADHD Survival Tips
🚨 Never skip breakfast. ADHD brains skip meals then binge. Eat within an hour of waking up — even just eggs or Greek yogurt.
🍗 Protein at every window. Protein keeps you full and kills cravings. Aim for 25-30g per meal.
💧 Drink water before you eat. Thirst mimics hunger. 16oz before each meal = fewer calories eaten naturally.
🧠 Prep one thing Sunday. Hard boil 6 eggs. Cook a batch of chicken. Having protein ready kills impulsive eating.
🚫 Liquid calories wreck this. Juice, Starbucks drinks, soda — they eat your calories without filling you up. Water, black coffee, unsweet tea only.
